01/The System

One idea in. One posted short out.
Five stations in between.

Every Blind short ran the same relay. Each tool does the one thing it’s best at; I do the judgment in between — the brief, the taste, the final cut.

1 ChatGPTWrite Scripts, hooks, captions, copy — drafted, punched up, and A/B-tested in minutes, not days.
2 Google GeminiVisualize Characters, frames, and thumbnails, generated to a brief — with looks that stay consistent across a series.
3 HiggsfieldAnimate Stills become cinema. Camera-controlled AI video — dolly, orbit, crane — picking the best of today’s video models for each shot.
4 SunoScore Original music and audio hooks: jingles, parody tracks, and full “Beats by Blind” songs written to the script.
5 Figma + AdobeFinish Captions, timing, assembly — the human 10% that makes the AI 90% feel intentional. (Adobe Certified Professional ×2)

The point isn’t the tools — it’s that the system let one person ship like a team.
